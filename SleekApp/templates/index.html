About

Sleek was built in order to explore and experiment with the capabilities of machine learning, and the true depth of the field. We wanted to simplify the lives of people, condensing bloated and unnecessarily long news stories to concise summarizations.

Depth

Sleek was designed in order to address the Depth track for Hack3. According to Merriem-Webster the word depth means the degree of intensity. Oftentimes News articles from places like BBC or CNN go into extensive detail on global issues. Issues like these inform and educate the public on pressing issues. However, in this current age most individuals create their opinions off of the title rather than the actual content of the article. These titles often over-exaggerate or polarize the issue, but due to the wordy and intense content of these articles the public often avoids actual consumption of the topic. In order to combat the misinformation spreading due to a lack of willingness to read Sleek simplifies the articles and creates a more digestable snapshot of the issue following the trend of the modern short-form content era.
